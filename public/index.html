Thank you for submitting your meeting transcript to Periscope and welcome to your meeting report. This report is based on the Polaris Formula™, a derivative of the Fast Teaming Formula®, documented in the book Build Better Teams, authored by George Karseras, and endorsed by Amy Edmondson.

The Polaris Formula™ is a simple and practical way to generate more value from any meeting you lead or attend. We PORT-In to ensure meetings start well and we PORT-Out to ensure they end well, and in between we exercise SAFE skills to generate plenty of psychological safety and RACE skills to enable excellent decision making efficiency.

This report summarises your SAFE RACE to PORT® skills and extracts further data to enable you to identify how you can improve Return on Time Invested (ROTI) at any meeting you lead or attend.
